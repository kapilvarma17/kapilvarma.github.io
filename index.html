Masters in Computer Science and Engineering • Sept 2018 - March 2020

After Finishing my Bachelors, I desired to explore more in the field of computer science. What better than pursuing my masters in the place where the start-ups take birth? That’s right, I came to Silicon Valley to pursue my master’s in Computer Science and Engineering. I have taken courses such as Design and Analysis of Algorithms, Operating Systems, Computer Architecture, Mobile Application Development, Software Process Development Management, Wireless and Multi Networks. One of my personal projects that I worked on during my mastersis an Android app that notifies the user with health-related tips based on their settings using Firebase. SVKM's NMIMS Mukesh Patel School of Technology Management and Engineering

Bachelor of Technology in Computer Engineering• August 2014 - May 2018

Bachelors was an amazing ride of my life where I learned and explored variety of fields. I worked on a system for attendance tracking. The technologies used for Back-end were Java Servlets, SQL. Front-end used HTML, CSS, Bootstrap. Database used was MySQL. The purpose of this project was to design and automate the process of taking student attendance as well as provide analytics at the end of each semester. My major final year undergraduate project was basically a system called ‘Trace’ that tracks the child’s location on his/her entry/exit into or from the school as well as their location on the bus using RFID, GPS/GSM module, Raspberry Pi and Android. The technologies used Android programming, Python, Embedded C, C# (Visual Studio). I also take a keen interest in beta testing of iOS and Android software development for bug fixing as well as for testing these software’s before they are deployed to the users.
